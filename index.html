Every Drop Counts!


Our Mission

Hi reader! We are a group of college students who are passionate about water conservation and sustainability. While water is a recyclable and infinite resource, there is a limited source. The quicker we use up our clean water sources, the quicker they will deplete and put all of us into danger. This is why we created a website to spread awareness about daily water usage and how you can start conserving water. In this website, you will find a water usage calculator to measure your daily water usage and a resources page with tips on how to conserve water in your every day life. Thank you for visiting and we hope you learn more about how you can help us contribute to a more sustainable world!
